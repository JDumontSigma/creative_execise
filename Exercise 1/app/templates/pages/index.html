Why did I choose this

I chose this idea due to the range of elements that it is built up of, allowing me to utilise the various features that canvas has to offer, ranging from drawing straight lines to bezier curves.

Another beneficial reason to develop this scenary is the duplication of objects, allowing me to further develop the product with the use of functions.

I chose to use sunset as the time period for this drawing as I was then able to try out the gradient function, to provide a scenic back drop.

Finally, as the shapes were complex and built up of a range of colours it allowed me to understand how elements are built ontop of one another whilst changing the colour and stroke of each shape. Plus, with the scenary setup I have chosen it opens itself to being used for the animation drawing.
